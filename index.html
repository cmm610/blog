About Me

I, like many others, came from a different background other than CS. I got my degree in Mechanical Engineering and after working in the field for a few years, I decided to switch to web development. I realized that if I stayed on the path that I was on, my path would become predictable. While this wouldn't have been the worst thing in the world, I decided that it was time to learn something which I felt was the way of the future: programming!

I started off learning from a friend of mine, but when he told me he was leaving for LA, I decided to pick up and move from Montana so that I could continue my education. I've been in LA now for 3.5 years, and I haven't looked back since I left.

Besides coding, I am interested in a variety of activities. They include exercising, in the form of going to the gym or riding my bicycle, and being creative with art, food and crafts. The crafting I do includes woodworking projects. I've built a number of things, including some night stands, a lofted bed, and now a murphy bed. I also love to sew and I've made my Jessie from Toy Story, Katarina from LOL, and superhero costumes.
